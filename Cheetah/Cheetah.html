What is cheetah

The cheetah is a large cat and the fastest land animal. It has a tawny to creamy white or pale buff fur that is marked with evenly spaced, solid black spots.The head is small and rounded,with a short snout and black tear-like facial streaks.

Cheetah's speed

Cheetah is the fastest land animal and the second fast animal after Falcon peregrine in whole World. Cheetahs are the fastest animals in the world reaching speeds of up to 120kph or 75mph.They are not only fast but also have amazing acceleration. Researchers found they can increase their speed by 10kph in a single stride.

Cheetah; a predator

Cheetahs also make their mark on any ecosystem they occupy simply by being large predators at or near the top of food chain. The cheetah's main food source in the wild where they live is Thomson's gazelle. Wherever there are high concentrations of Thomson's gazelle, there will be high density of cheetahs.
